Thousand Oaks Overview

Thousand Oaks is a premier city in the Conejo Valley, known for its scenic beauty, master-planned communities, and exceptional quality of life. Consistently ranked among the safest cities in America, Thousand Oaks offers a perfect blend of suburban comfort and natural splendor.

The city derives its name from the abundance of oak trees that dot the landscape, creating a picturesque setting against the backdrop of the Santa Monica Mountains. With over 15,000 acres of publicly owned open space, residents enjoy access to numerous parks, hiking trails, and recreational areas.

Thousand Oaks is home to thriving business districts, cultural venues like the Civic Arts Plaza, and excellent educational institutions. The city's commitment to controlled growth and environmental preservation has maintained its charming character while providing modern amenities and services.

Thousand Oaks Neighborhoods

Thousand Oaks features a diverse range of neighborhoods, each with its own distinct character and appeal. From established communities to newer developments, the city offers housing options to suit various preferences and lifestyles.

Lynn Ranch

Spacious Estates and Timeless Charm Lynn Ranch offers a unique blend of rural charm and upscale living, with large custom homes on expansive lots, equestrian properties, and mature tree-lined streets. Known for its peaceful atmosphere, strong community feel, and proximity to top schools and outdoor recreation, Lynn Ranch is ideal for those seeking privacy, space, and classic California living—all within minutes of the heart of Thousand Oaks.

Wildwood

Tucked against the scenic backdrop of Wildwood Regional Park, the Wildwood neighborhood offers a peaceful, family-friendly lifestyle surrounded by hiking trails, open space, and breathtaking views. Known for its strong sense of community, top-rated schools, and charming suburban streets, Wildwood is perfect for those who crave outdoor adventure with the convenience of nearby shopping, dining, and freeway access.

Lang Ranch

A family-friendly neighborhood with well-maintained homes, excellent schools, and abundant recreational opportunities. Popular with families for its community amenities and parks.Nestled in the rolling hills with access to parks, hiking trails, and open space, Lang Ranch offers a perfect blend of luxury, nature, and family-friendly living—all just minutes from shopping, dining, and major commuter routes.

Dos Vientos

Dos Vientos Ranch is a highly sought-after master-planned community located in the southwestern part of Newbury Park, within the city of Thousand Oaks, California. Surrounded by the scenic beauty of the Santa Monica Mountains, this upscale neighborhood offers a peaceful, suburban lifestyle with a mix of Mediterranean and Spanish-style homes built between the late 1990s and early 2010s. Residents are drawn to Dos Vientos for its spacious single-family homes, townhomes, and estates, many of which feature modern amenities and larger lots. The community is known for its strong sense of safety and family-friendly atmosphere, making it a popular choice for families, professionals, and retirees alike.

Market Trends

The Thousand Oaks real estate market has shown consistent appreciation over the years, with limited inventory and high demand keeping property values strong. The city's geographic constraints and strict development regulations help maintain the exclusivity and desirability of its neighborhoods.

Luxury properties in areas like North Ranch and Westlake Hills command premium prices, while established neighborhoods offer more accessible options for families. The market for condominiums and townhomes remains active, particularly near commercial centers and transportation corridors.

Thousand Oaks Schools

Thousand Oaks is served by the highly-rated Conejo Valley Unified School District, known for its academic excellence and comprehensive programs. The area also offers several prestigious private school options and is home to California Lutheran University.

Public Schools

  • Westlake High School - Consistently ranked among the top high schools in California
  • Thousand Oaks High School - Known for strong academic and athletic programs
  • Redwood Middle School - Award-winning middle school with diverse program offerings
  • Conejo Elementary School - Elementary school with strong parent involvement

Private Schools

  • Oaks Christian School - College preparatory school serving grades 5-12
  • La Reina High School & Middle School - Catholic college preparatory school for girls
  • Ascension Lutheran School - Private school offering education from preschool through 8th grade

Higher Education

  • California Lutheran University - Private university offering undergraduate and graduate programs
  • Moorpark College - Community college located nearby in Moorpark
