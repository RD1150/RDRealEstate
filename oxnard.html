Welcome to Oxnard

Oxnard offers a unique coastal lifestyle with beautiful beaches, a rich agricultural heritage, and diverse neighborhoods. Known for its mild climate, vibrant cultural scene, and strong sense of community, this charming coastal city provides an exceptional quality of life along the stunning California coast.

About Oxnard

Located in Ventura County along the California coast, Oxnard is the largest city in the county and offers residents a perfect blend of urban amenities and coastal charm. The city is known for its beautiful beaches, including Oxnard Beach Park, Mandalay State Beach, and Silver Strand Beach, providing residents with abundant opportunities for surfing, swimming, and beachcombing.

Oxnard's rich agricultural heritage is evident in the vast strawberry fields and farmland that surround the city, earning it the nickname "Strawberry Capital of California." The city hosts the annual California Strawberry Festival, celebrating this important local crop and drawing visitors from throughout the region.

The city features a diverse range of housing options, from beachfront properties and marina-adjacent homes to family-friendly neighborhoods with tree-lined streets. Residents enjoy a variety of shopping and dining options at The Collection at RiverPark, historic downtown Oxnard, and Channel Islands Harbor, as well as cultural attractions like the Carnegie Art Museum and the Murphy Auto Museum.

Featured Neighborhoods

Channel Islands

Channel Islands is a prestigious waterfront community built around a series of navigable channels connected to the Channel Islands Harbor. This unique neighborhood offers residents the opportunity to dock boats right at their homes and enjoy a waterfront lifestyle.

Properties in Channel Islands typically range from $1.2 million to $3+ million, featuring homes with private docks, water views, and spacious layouts designed for indoor-outdoor living.

Oxnard Shores

Oxnard Shores is one of the most sought-after beachside communities in Oxnard, featuring homes just steps from the sand. This desirable neighborhood offers a relaxed beach lifestyle with easy access to Oxnard Beach Park and Mandalay State Beach.

Properties in Oxnard Shores typically range from $1 million to $3.5+ million, offering exceptional beachside living, ocean views, and outdoor spaces designed for enjoying the coastal climate.

River Park

River Park is a master-planned community in northeast Oxnard, offering a family-friendly atmosphere with tree-lined streets, parks, and excellent schools. This neighborhood is known for its strong sense of community and convenient access to shopping and dining at The Collection at RiverPark.

Properties in River Park typically range from $700,000 to $1.2+ million, featuring well-maintained homes with spacious yards and access to community amenities.

Oxnard Real Estate Market

The Oxnard real estate market remains strong, with consistent demand for properties in this desirable coastal city. The combination of beautiful beaches, perfect climate, and diverse housing options continues to attract buyers seeking a high quality of life in Southern California.

Current market trends include:

  • Premium prices for beachfront and harbor-view properties
  • Strong demand for homes in family-friendly neighborhoods
  • Growing interest in properties with outdoor living spaces
  • Increasing appeal to buyers looking for coastal living at more accessible price points than Malibu or Santa Barbara
  • Steady appreciation in property values across all neighborhoods
