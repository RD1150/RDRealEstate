Simi Valley, California

A perfect blend of suburban comfort, natural beauty, and family-friendly living

Explore Simi Valley Properties

Living in Simi Valley

Discover what makes Simi Valley one of Ventura County's most desirable communities

Shopping & Dining

Simi Valley Town Center and surrounding areas offer a variety of shopping options, from national retailers to local boutiques, along with diverse dining experiences for every taste.

Natural Beauty & Recreation

Surrounded by the Santa Susana Mountains and Simi Hills, the area offers miles of hiking trails, parks, and outdoor recreational facilities for active lifestyles.

Excellent Schools

Simi Valley Unified School District provides quality education with several highly-rated schools, making it an attractive location for families with children.

Safety & Security

Consistently ranked among the safest cities in Ventura County, Simi Valley offers peace of mind with low crime rates and responsive emergency services.

Strong Community

Simi Valley hosts numerous community events throughout the year, including the Simi Valley Days festival and concerts in the park, fostering connections among residents.

Convenient Location

Located just 40 minutes from downtown Los Angeles, Simi Valley offers an ideal balance of suburban tranquility with easy access to urban amenities and employment centers.

Simi Valley Neighborhoods

Explore the distinctive communities that make up this family-friendly city

Wood Ranch

A premier master-planned community featuring a variety of housing options, from luxury estates to townhomes, with beautiful landscaping, mountain views, and access to the Wood Ranch Golf Club.

Big Sky

A newer community featuring contemporary homes with modern amenities and stunning mountain views, offering a convenient location near shopping, dining, and recreational facilities.

Santa Susana Knolls

A unique area offering a rural atmosphere with larger lots and a variety of architectural styles, providing a more secluded setting with natural surroundings and wildlife.

Texas Tract

An established neighborhood with charming homes on spacious lots, offering a family-friendly environment with tree-lined streets and a strong sense of community.

Bridle Path

An equestrian-friendly neighborhood featuring larger properties with horse facilities, offering a semi-rural lifestyle while still being close to urban amenities.

Simi Hills

A desirable area with a mix of housing styles, many offering panoramic views of the surrounding hills and valleys, with convenient access to golf courses and recreational facilities.

Simi Valley Real Estate Market

Current trends and statistics for this desirable Ventura County community

The Simi Valley real estate market remains strong, with consistent demand for properties in this desirable area. The combination of excellent schools, safe neighborhoods, and convenient location continues to attract buyers seeking a high quality of life in Ventura County.

Current market trends include steady appreciation in property values, strong demand for homes in top school districts, growing interest in properties with larger lots and outdoor spaces, increasing appeal to buyers looking for more space outside of Los Angeles, and new construction developments to meet growing demand.
