Living in Malibu

Discover what makes Malibu one of Southern California's most prestigious coastal communities

World-Class Beaches

Malibu is home to some of California's most beautiful beaches, including Zuma Beach, Surfrider Beach, and Paradise Cove, offering pristine sands and legendary surf breaks.

Exceptional Dining

From oceanfront seafood at Nobu and Mastro's Ocean Club to casual fare at Malibu Farm Pier Cafe, the city offers world-class dining options with stunning coastal views.

Outdoor Recreation

Surrounded by the Santa Monica Mountains, Malibu offers miles of hiking trails, mountain biking paths, horseback riding, and water sports for outdoor enthusiasts.

Privacy & Exclusivity

Known for its secluded estates and gated communities, Malibu offers unparalleled privacy and security, making it a preferred location for celebrities and executives.

Arts & Culture

Home to the Getty Villa, Pepperdine University's Weisman Museum, and numerous galleries, Malibu offers rich cultural experiences in a stunning coastal setting.

Luxury Lifestyle

From high-end shopping at Malibu Country Mart to exclusive beach clubs and wellness retreats, Malibu offers a refined coastal lifestyle unlike any other.

Malibu Neighborhoods

Explore the distinctive communities that make up this iconic coastal city

Carbon Beach

Carbon Beach, often referred to as “Billionaire’s Beach,” is Malibu’s most iconic and prestigious stretch of sand. Located just off Pacific Coast Highway near the Malibu Pier and Nobu, this ultra-exclusive enclave is home to some of the most expensive oceanfront real estate in the world. With only a limited number of beachfront properties, each estate boasts private beach access, unrivaled views of the Pacific, and world-class architecture. Residents enjoy total privacy while being steps from Malibu’s finest dining, shopping, and cultural hotspots. Carbon Beach isn’t just a neighborhood — it’s a legacy address for those seeking the pinnacle of luxury coastal living.

Malibu Colony

Malibu Colony is one of Malibu’s most historic and exclusive gated communities, often dubbed the "original Malibu celebrity enclave." Situated along a pristine stretch of beach just south of the Malibu Pier, this ultra-private neighborhood features a limited number of beachfront estates, many with direct sand access and breathtaking ocean views. Since the Golden Age of Hollywood, Malibu Colony has attracted A-listers, executives, and discerning homeowners seeking luxury, privacy, and the ultimate beachfront lifestyle. With 24/7 guarded gates and a strong sense of community, it's the perfect blend of prestige, security, and iconic coastal charm — all just moments from world-class restaurants, shops, and the Malibu Country Mart.

Point Dume

Point Dume is one of Malibu’s most coveted neighborhoods, perched atop dramatic ocean bluffs with sweeping views of the Pacific, Catalina Island, and beyond. Known for its spacious lots, private beach access, and natural beauty, Point Dume offers a rare blend of coastal elegance and laid-back charm. Residents enjoy access to scenic hiking trails, secluded coves like Little Dume Beach, and a tight-knit, upscale community. With a mix of ranch-style homes and multimillion-dollar estates, it’s a favorite for those seeking privacy, space, and a true connection to Malibu’s surf-and-sand lifestyle. Whether you're watching dolphins from your deck or catching waves before breakfast, Point Dume delivers the ultimate in California coastal living.

Broad Beach

Broad Beach is a prestigious Malibu neighborhood known for its wide sandy shoreline, luxurious beachfront estates, and laid-back elegance. Stretching along the western end of Malibu, this sought-after enclave offers a rare combination of privacy, space, and natural beauty. Homes here range from contemporary architectural masterpieces to classic Cape Cod-style retreats, many with direct beach access and sweeping ocean views. Though tucked away and peaceful, Broad Beach is just minutes from Trancas Country Market, gourmet dining, and top-rated schools. It’s a favorite among high-profile residents and families alike, offering a true Malibu lifestyle with space to breathe.

Serra Retreat

Serra Retreat is one of Malibu’s most private and prestigious enclaves, tucked behind gates just off Pacific Coast Highway and moments from Surfrider Beach and the Malibu Country Mart. Surrounded by mature sycamores and lush greenery, this serene neighborhood offers a rare blend of luxury, nature, and seclusion. The community is known for its sprawling estates, equestrian properties, and celebrity residents — all nestled within a tranquil canyon setting. With 24/7 guarded gates and limited public access, Serra Retreat provides unmatched privacy and a peaceful, spiritual atmosphere, thanks in part to the historic Serra Retreat Center at its heart. It’s the ideal sanctuary for those seeking Malibu living with a sense of quiet sophistication.

Malibu West

A Located just past Zuma Beach along the iconic Pacific Coast Highway, Malibu West offers one of the most accessible entry points into the coveted Malibu lifestyle. “This is the most affordable neighborhood in Malibu,” says Sandro Dazzan, managing partner at The Agency Malibu and a lifelong local. “It’s a favorite for families thanks to its starter homes, safety, and strong sense of community — there’s even a guard gate at the top of the neighborhood.” With a CAP Index Crime Score of just 1 out of 10 (compared to the national average of 4), it’s not uncommon to see kids riding bikes and skateboards or families out walking their dogs..
