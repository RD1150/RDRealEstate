Welcome to Conejo Valley

Nestled between the Santa Monica Mountains and the Simi Hills, the Conejo Valley is a picturesque region known for its stunning landscapes, excellent schools, and high quality of life. This beautiful area encompasses several communities including Thousand Oaks, Newbury Park, Westlake Village,Simi Valley,and Moorpark offering residents a perfect blend of suburban comfort and natural beauty.Please see communities tab for a full list and more detailed information about all of these towns.

About Conejo Valley

The Conejo Valley, with its name derived from the Spanish word for "rabbit," has transformed from a rural landscape to a thriving suburban region while maintaining its natural charm. The area is characterized by rolling hills, oak-studded landscapes, and carefully planned communities that blend harmoniously with the surrounding environment.

Residents enjoy a mild Mediterranean climate with warm, dry summers and mild winters, making it ideal for outdoor activities year-round. The region's commitment to preserving open spaces has resulted in numerous parks, hiking trails, and recreational areas that contribute to the exceptional quality of life in the Conejo Valley.

Conejo Valley Communities

Thousand Oaks

As the largest city in the Conejo Valley, Thousand Oaks offers a diverse range of housing options from luxury estates to family-friendly neighborhoods. Known for its excellent schools, low crime rates, and abundant parks, Thousand Oaks has consistently ranked among the safest cities in America. The city features the popular Janss Marketplace and The Oaks shopping mall, along with the Civic Arts Plaza which hosts Broadway shows and concerts.

Newbury Park

Located at the western end of the Conejo Valley, Newbury Park offers a more relaxed suburban feel with beautiful mountain views and proximity to outdoor recreation areas. The community features a mix of established neighborhoods and newer developments, with housing options ranging from condominiums to spacious single-family homes. Residents enjoy easy access to Boney Mountain and the Santa Monica Mountains National Recreation Area.

Westlake Village

Straddling the Los Angeles and Ventura County line, Westlake Village is an upscale community centered around the picturesque Westlake Lake. Known for its meticulously planned development, the area features luxury homes, exclusive gated communities, and premium amenities. Residents enjoy the Westlake Promenade shopping center, the Four Seasons Hotel, and numerous country clubs and golf courses that contribute to the area's resort-like atmosphere.

Conejo Valley Real Estate Market

The Conejo Valley real estate market is known for its stability and strong property values, supported by excellent schools, low crime rates, and high quality of life. The region offers diverse housing options ranging from condominiums and townhomes to luxury estates, catering to various lifestyles and budgets.

Key market characteristics include:

  • Strong demand for family homes in top school districts
  • Premium values for properties with mountain or lake views
  • Steady appreciation rates compared to neighboring Los Angeles
  • Limited inventory of new construction due to careful growth management
  • Diverse architectural styles from mid-century modern to Mediterranean

Whether you're looking to buy or sell in the Conejo Valley, having a knowledgeable local real estate expert is essential to navigate this competitive market successfully.
