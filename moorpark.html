Moorpark

A Charming Blend of Rural Beauty and Modern Convenience in Ventura County

Living in Moorpark

Explore what makes Moorpark a desirable place to live

Recreation

Enjoy numerous outdoor activities at Arroyo Vista Community Park, Moorpark Country Club's championship golf course, America's Teaching Zoo at Moorpark College, and nearby hiking trails in the Santa Monica Mountains.

Dining & Shopping

Explore Moorpark Marketplace with a variety of shops and restaurants, Historic High Street with charming boutiques and eateries, and family-owned restaurants serving diverse cuisines.

Strategic Location

Located approximately 50 miles northwest of Los Angeles, 20 minutes to Thousand Oaks, and 25 minutes to beaches in Ventura, with easy access to Highway 118 and Highway 23, plus a Metrolink station.

Education

Moorpark is served by the highly-rated Moorpark Unified School District, with excellent schools consistently ranking among the best in Ventura County, plus Moorpark College offering higher education opportunities.

Community Events

Enjoy seasonal events like the Country Days Parade, 3rd of July Fireworks Extravaganza, Moorpark Farmers Market, and various community gatherings that foster a strong sense of belonging.

Safety & Security

Moorpark consistently ranks among the safest cities in Ventura County, with low crime rates, active neighborhood watch programs, and responsive emergency services providing peace of mind for residents.

Housing in Moorpark

Discover the diverse real estate options available in this desirable community

Architectural Styles

Homes in Moorpark showcase a range of architectural styles, from Mediterranean and Spanish Colonial to Contemporary and Traditional designs, with thoughtfully planned developments and cohesive aesthetics.

Single-Family Homes

Choose from established and newer neighborhoods offering single-family homes with various lot sizes, floor plans, and amenities to suit different family needs and preferences.

Townhomes & Condos

Enjoy low-maintenance living with townhomes and condominiums that offer amenities like community pools, fitness centers, and landscaped common areas in convenient locations.

Luxury Estates

Discover upscale properties on larger lots with premium features, mountain views, and custom designs, often located in prestigious neighborhoods or gated communities.

Ranch Properties

Experience rural living with ranch properties and equestrian estates in the outskirts of Moorpark, offering larger acreage, privacy, and space for horses and other animals.

New Construction

Explore newly built homes and developments featuring modern designs, energy-efficient features, smart home technology, and the latest amenities for contemporary living.

Moorpark Neighborhoods

Explore the distinctive communities that make up this charming city

Mountain Meadows

An established neighborhood featuring larger homes on spacious lots with beautiful mountain views and proximity to Mountain Meadows Golf Course.

Campus Park

A family-friendly community with a mix of single-family homes and townhouses, located near Moorpark College and offering excellent amenities.

Country Club Estates

Upscale homes surrounding the Moorpark Country Club, featuring luxury properties with golf course views and resort-style living.

Peach Hill

A well-established neighborhood with mature landscaping, larger lots, and a mix of architectural styles, offering beautiful views and a peaceful atmosphere.

Downtown Moorpark

Historic area centered around High Street, featuring a mix of older homes with character, newer developments, and easy access to local shops and restaurants.

Varsity Park

A newer development with modern homes, family-friendly amenities, community parks, and convenient access to schools and shopping centers.

What Makes Agoura Hills Special

Discover the unique features that make this community a desirable place to call home

Natural Beauty

Surrounded by the Santa Monica Mountains National Recreation Area, Agoura Hills offers residents unparalleled access to hiking trails, nature preserves, and outdoor activities. The city is committed to preserving its natural environment, with numerous parks and open spaces.

Excellent Schools

Agoura Hills is served by the highly-rated Las Virgenes Unified School District, which consistently ranks among the top districts in California. The area's excellent public and private schools make it a top choice for families prioritizing education.

Vibrant Lifestyle

Residents enjoy a diverse range of dining, shopping, and entertainment options. The city hosts community events throughout the year, including the popular Reyes Adobe Days festival, farmers markets, and outdoor concerts, fostering a strong sense of community.

Agoura Hills Real Estate Market

Current trends and insights into the local housing market

The Agoura Hills real estate market remains strong, with steady appreciation and high demand for homes in this desirable community. The median home price in Agoura Hills is approximately $1.2 million, with luxury properties in premier neighborhoods commanding significantly higher prices.

Properties in Agoura Hills typically sell for around $550 per square foot, with homes staying on the market for an average of 32 days. The list-to-sale price ratio is approximately 98.2%, indicating a competitive market where sellers often receive close to their asking price.

The market is particularly strong for single-family homes with updated features, outdoor living spaces, and views. Homes in top school districts and those with larger lots continue to be in high demand, often receiving multiple offers.
