Homes We Specialize In

At Reena Dutta Real Estate, we specialize in a diverse range of properties throughout Ventura County and the surrounding areas. Whether you're looking for a luxury estate, a family home in a top school district, or an investment property, our expertise spans across various property types and price points.

Our deep knowledge of the local real estate market, combined with our personalized approach, ensures that we can help you find the perfect property that meets your specific needs and lifestyle preferences.

Property Types We Specialize In

Luxury Homes

Exquisite properties featuring premium amenities, high-end finishes, and exceptional craftsmanship in prestigious locations.

Family Homes

Comfortable and spacious residences in family-friendly neighborhoods with excellent schools and community amenities.

Estates

Expansive properties with acreage, privacy, and often featuring guest houses, equestrian facilities, or vineyards.

Low-maintenance living options with amenities such as pools, fitness centers, and security, perfect for busy professionals or downsizers.

Investment Properties

New Construction

Brand new homes featuring modern designs, energy-efficient systems, and the latest amenities in developing communities.
