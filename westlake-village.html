Area Overview

Westlake Village is a planned community that straddles the Los Angeles and Ventura county line. The eastern portion is the incorporated city of Westlake Village, located on the western edge of Los Angeles County, California. The city, located in the region known as the Conejo Valley, encompasses half of the area surrounding Westlake Lake, and small portions of Hidden Valley and North Ranch.

The original community was known simply as "Westlake". Roughly two-thirds of it was annexed by the city of Thousand Oaks in two portions, in 1968 and 1972. In 1981, the remaining third eventually incorporated as the City of Westlake Village.

Westlake Village is known for its affluent and secluded character, beautiful lake, and numerous amenities including shopping centers, golf courses, and hiking trails. The community is home to the headquarters of the Dole Food Company, K-Swiss, and other corporations.

Neighborhoods

North Ranch

North Ranch is an exclusive gated community located in the northern part of Westlake Village. It features luxury homes, many with stunning views of the surrounding mountains and valleys. The neighborhood is adjacent to the North Ranch Country Club, which offers a championship golf course, tennis courts, and fine dining.

Westlake Island

Westlake Island is a private, gated island community located in the middle of Westlake Lake. This exclusive neighborhood features luxury waterfront homes with private boat docks. Residents enjoy 24-hour security, beautiful views, and direct access to the lake for boating, fishing, and other water activities.

First Neighborhood

First Neighborhood was the original neighborhood developed in Westlake Village. It features well-maintained homes, tree-lined streets, and a strong sense of community. The neighborhood includes parks, walking paths, a community center, and a pool. It's known for its family-friendly atmosphere and convenient location near schools and shopping.

Westlake Hills

Westlake Hills is one of the most established and sought-after neighborhoods in Westlake Village, California. Known for its mature trees, spacious lots, and charming, custom-built homes, Westlake Hills offers a peaceful, suburban feel while still being close to modern amenities. This neighborhood is especially popular among families thanks to its top-rated public schools, including Westlake Hills Elementary, and its proximity to parks and hiking trails like Triunfo Canyon Park. Homes here typically range from traditional single-story ranch styles to larger two-story residences, many of which have been beautifully remodeled. Westlake Hills is also just minutes from The Promenade at Westlake, fine dining, coffee shops, and easy freeway access—making it a prime location for those seeking a balance of convenience, community, and natural beauty. Lake Sherwood

Lake Sherwood is a prestigious, guard-gated community nestled in the hills just south of Westlake Village, built around the stunning Lake Sherwood reservoir. Known for its privacy, natural beauty, and luxury lifestyle, this exclusive enclave features custom-built estates with breathtaking views of the lake, golf course, and surrounding mountains. Residents enjoy access to the renowned Lake Sherwood Country Club, home to a Jack Nicklaus-designed golf course, tennis courts, and a private clubhouse. With 24/7 security and a serene, resort-like atmosphere, Lake Sherwood offers a peaceful retreat that remains just minutes from the shops, dining, and conveniences of Westlake Village. It's a sought-after haven for those looking to blend elegance, nature, and privacy in one of Southern California’s most elite neighborhoods.

The Trails

The Trails is known for its custom estate-style homes nestled among tree-lined streets and rolling hills. The neighborhood exudes a timeless charm, with architecture that reflects a blend of traditional and contemporary designs. Many properties feature expansive lots, offering privacy and a serene environment. The community's proximity to Westlake Lake and local amenities makes it a sought-after location for those seeking both tranquility and convenience.

South Shore Hills

Southshore Hills is a peaceful, well-established neighborhood located near the southern edge of Westlake Lake in Westlake Village, California. Known for its wide, tree-lined streets and larger lots, the area offers a blend of privacy, natural beauty, and convenience. Built mainly in the 1970s and 1980s, homes in Southshore Hills feature a variety of architectural styles—from classic ranch to updated Mediterranean—and many have been beautifully remodeled. Properties often include spacious yards, private pools, and scenic views of the Santa Monica Mountains or glimpses of the lake. Residents enjoy walking distance to Westlake Lake, top-rated schools, and destinations like The Landing and The Promenade at Westlake. Outdoor lovers appreciate the proximity to trails and parks such as Triunfo Canyon. With no HOA and a strong sense of community, Southshore Hills offers relaxed, upscale living in one of the most desirable parts of Westlake Village.

Market Trends

The Westlake Village real estate market has shown consistent appreciation over the past decade, with particularly strong growth in recent years. Limited inventory and high demand continue to drive a competitive market, especially for properties in the most desirable neighborhoods and school districts.

Luxury properties, particularly those with lake or mountain views, command significant premiums. The market for condominiums and townhomes remains strong, particularly among first-time buyers and those looking to downsize.
