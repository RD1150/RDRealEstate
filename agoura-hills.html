About Agoura Hills

A charming community with a perfect blend of suburban comfort and natural beauty

Nestled in the foothills of the Santa Monica Mountains, Agoura Hills is a picturesque community known for its stunning natural landscapes, excellent schools, and high quality of life. With a perfect blend of suburban comfort and natural beauty, Agoura Hills offers residents a peaceful retreat while maintaining convenient access to the greater Los Angeles area.

Originally settled by the Chumash Indians, the area was later part of the Rancho Las Virgenes land grant. The city was incorporated in 1982 and has since developed into a thriving community that values its natural surroundings and small-town charm.

Today, Agoura Hills is home to approximately 20,000 residents who enjoy the city's excellent amenities, outdoor recreation opportunities, and strong sense of community. The area is particularly known for its excellent public schools, making it a popular choice for families.

What Makes Agoura Hills Special

Discover the unique features that make this community a desirable place to call home

Natural Beauty

Surrounded by the Santa Monica Mountains National Recreation Area, Agoura Hills offers residents unparalleled access to hiking trails, nature preserves, and outdoor activities. The city is committed to preserving its natural environment, with numerous parks and open spaces.

Excellent Schools

Agoura Hills is served by the highly-rated Las Virgenes Unified School District, which consistently ranks among the top districts in California. The area's excellent public and private schools make it a top choice for families prioritizing education.

Vibrant Lifestyle

Residents enjoy a diverse range of dining, shopping, and entertainment options. The city hosts community events throughout the year, including the popular Reyes Adobe Days festival, farmers markets, and outdoor concerts, fostering a strong sense of community.

Agoura Hills Real Estate Market

Current trends and insights into the local housing market

The Agoura Hills real estate market remains strong, with steady appreciation and high demand for homes in this desirable community. The median home price in Agoura Hills is approximately $1.2 million, with luxury properties in premier neighborhoods commanding significantly higher prices.

Properties in Agoura Hills typically sell for around $550 per square foot, with homes staying on the market for an average of 32 days. The list-to-sale price ratio is approximately 98.2%, indicating a competitive market where sellers often receive close to their asking price.

The market is particularly strong for single-family homes with updated features, outdoor living spaces, and views. Homes in top school districts and those with larger lots continue to be in high demand, often receiving multiple offers.
